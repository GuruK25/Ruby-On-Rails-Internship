Associations: Associations are nothing but creating the relationship between the tables.

In rails, There are 4 types of Associations
OneToOne
OneToMany
ManyToOne
ManyToMany

In rails, We can establish the associations in 7 different ways.
1. HasOne
2. HasMany
3. BelongsTo
4. HasOneThrough
5. HasManyThrough
6. HasManyBelongsTo


7. Polymorphic associations(different from above)
